How it started

One boring sock drawer, one tiny rebellion

It began with a simple question staring up from a drawer full of grey, black, and "is-this-navy-or-black": when did socks get so boring? We figured the bit of you nobody plans around deserves the most personality.

So we started Oddpeel — named for our patron banana, peeling back the dull to reveal something brighter underneath. Every pattern is drawn in-house, knitted (never printed), and tested on real feet through real laundry cycles.

Today a small studio of certified weirdos designs every drop. Same obsession, more colors.
