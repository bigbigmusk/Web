Everything about our objects, materials, shipping and care.

Materials & Care

What are the objects made of?

Recycled 925 sterling silver and ethically sourced natural stones — lapis lazuli, agate, smoky quartz, lava and river stone. Each object is finished by hand, so small variations are intended and unique to your piece.

How do I care for silver and stone?

Keep dry and store flat, away from other metals. Polish gently with a soft cloth. Avoid perfume, lotion and water on the silver. For fragrance pieces, scent only the porous stone — never the metal.

Will the silver develop a patina?

Yes — and we welcome it. Silver warms and softens with wear. If you prefer a bright finish, a silver cloth restores shine in moments.

Shipping & Returns

When will my order ship?

Objects are made to order and ship within 5–7 business days. You will receive a tracking note by email when your piece leaves the studio.

Do you offer free shipping?

Complimentary shipping on all orders over $199. Below that, a flat rate is calculated at inquiry.

What is your returns policy?

Unworn objects may be returned within 14 days of delivery for exchange or store credit. Made-to-order and personalised pieces are final sale. Contact us first and we will guide you.

Size Guide

What chain lengths do you offer?

Most necklaces ship at 50–55 cm with an adjustable clasp. Each product page lists exact pendant and chain dimensions under Details.

How do I size a bracelet?

Our silver bands are open and gently adjustable to most wrists (inner width ~58 mm). Shape with both hands; avoid repeated bending.

Orders & Tracking

How do I place an order?

Add objects to your cart, then choose Inquiry by email or WhatsApp at checkout. We confirm availability, share a secure payment link, and begin making your piece.

How do I track my order?

Reply to your confirmation email or message us via the Contact page with your name — we will share live status.
