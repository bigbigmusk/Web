FAQ

Quick answers

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 1 business day. Standard US delivery is 3–5 business days and free over $35. International usually arrives in 7–14 business days.

What sizes do your socks come in?

Most styles are one stretchy size fitting US men's 6–13 / women's 7–14. The exact fit is noted on each product page.

What's your return policy?

A 30-day happiness guarantee. Not happy? We'll replace or refund — and you usually don't need to ship the socks back.

How should I wash them?

Machine wash cold, inside out, tumble dry low. Our colors are knitted in, not printed, so they stay loud for the long haul.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship to most countries. Duties and taxes are calculated at checkout where applicable.

Do you offer wholesale?

We do. Email wholesale@oddpeel.com with your shop details and we'll send our line sheet.
