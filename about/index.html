About Concord Trade

Concord Trade is a B2B international sourcing and trade solutions company helping global buyers source reliable manufacturers, compare supplier options, and reduce sourcing risk across China and Asia.

Our Services Start a Sourcing Project

A sourcing partner, not just a supplier

Concord Trade connects international businesses with reliable manufacturers across China and Asia. Our role is not only to find products — it is to help clients reduce sourcing risk, improve communication efficiency, compare supplier options, and build stable supply partnerships.

We support the full sourcing journey: requirement analysis, supplier matching and verification, quotation comparison, OEM/ODM project management, sample coordination, quality control, and export documentation.
