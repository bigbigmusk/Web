Our roots

Born on the high plateau, carried across the world

Himalaya Atelier began with a simple wish — to keep the symbols, craft, and quiet wisdom of mountain life alive in objects you can wear every day. We work directly with nomadic artisans, honour the meaning behind each motif, and give a share of every sale back to the communities who inspire us.

From the eight-spoked wheel to the endless knot, every design is a small reminder to move through the world with intention.
