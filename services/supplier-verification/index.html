FAQ

Supplier verification — FAQ

What does supplier verification include?

It typically includes business license and registration checks, capability and product-fit assessment, a review of certifications and references, and sample validation. The goal is to confirm a supplier is real, capable, and a good match before you place an order.

Can you verify a supplier I already found?

Yes. Send us the supplier details and what you plan to order, and we will assess them and flag risks before you commit.

How does verification reduce sourcing risk?

Most sourcing problems come from choosing the wrong supplier. Verifying capability, legitimacy, and quality up front prevents wasted samples, failed orders, and quality disputes later.

Ready to start a sourcing project?

Tell us your product, target market, and volume. We'll find and verify suppliers, collect quotes, and follow up on samples, quality, and delivery.
