Medical consumables sourcing — FAQ

What medical consumables can you source?

Disposable gloves, face masks, and general medical disposables and PPE, plus related packaging. We focus on supplier verification and compliance-relevant documentation for your destination market.

Do you support compliance and certifications?

We help coordinate the certifications and documentation relevant to your market (for example CE or FDA-related requirements) with the manufacturer. Buyers remain responsible for confirming regulatory requirements in their country.

How is quality controlled for medical disposables?

Through supplier verification, clear quality and specification alignment, sample validation, and inspection follow-up before shipment.
