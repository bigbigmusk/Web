FAQ

China product sourcing — FAQ

How do you find reliable manufacturers in China?

We match your requirement against our supplier network and shortlist qualified manufacturers based on capability, product fit, compliance, and track record. We then collect and compare quotations and verify suppliers before recommending them.

What information do you need to start sourcing?

Share the product, target market, target quantity, target price, packaging requirements, and any certification needs. The more detail you give, the faster and more accurate the supplier matching and quotations.

Do you charge buyers a fee?

Engagement terms depend on the scope of the project. Tell us what you need and we will explain the most suitable way to work together before any commitment.

Can you handle small or trial orders?

Yes. We support flexible MOQs and trial orders where possible, and negotiate MOQ with manufacturers as part of OEM/ODM and sampling.

Ready to start a sourcing project?

Tell us your product, target market, and volume. We'll find and verify suppliers, collect quotes, and follow up on samples, quality, and delivery.
